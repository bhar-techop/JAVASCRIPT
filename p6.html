OPERATORS

Javascript operators are symbols that are used to perform operations on operands.

        1.Arithmetic operators=> Addition +,Substraction -,Multiplication *,Division /,Modulus %,Increment ++,Decrement --
        2. Comparision (Relational) Operators=> Is equal to ==,Identical (equal and of same data type) ===, Not equal to !=,Not Identical !==,Greater than >,Greater equal to >=,less than <,<= less than or equal to
        3. Bitwise Operators => Bitwise AND &,Bitwise OR  |,Bitwise XOR ^,Bitwise Not ~,Bitwise Left Shift <<,
        4. Logical Operators => Logical AND &&,Logical  OR ||,Logical Not !
        5. Assignment Operators =>Assign,Add and Assign +=,Substract and Assign-=,Multiply and Assign*=,Divide and Assign/=,Modulus and Assign%=
        6. Special or Ternary Operators => condition?"true":"false";
